Tab Space

Save browser tabs as a workspace.

Tab Space is an extension app which saves tabs, manages saved sessions and provides some useful shortcuts for Safari.

Changelog
Requires macOS 10.13 or later

Manage your tabs

Tab Space saves your Safari tabs as separate sessions. You can add tags to better organize these tabs. Restore them whenever you want.

Additional shortcuts

Use keyboard shortcuts to make your web browser smoother. And cooperate with other popular applications.

Dark Mode ready

Tab Space moves to dark mode automatically with your system theme's change.
